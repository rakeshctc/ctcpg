Chennai Trekking Club Present's - Chennai Trail Marathon 2014

About Chennai Trekking Club.

Chennai Trekking Club is a large non-profit group of enthusiastic people who share a common passion of outdoors. With around 20,000 members in google group and 8,500 members on Facebook it is one of the largest outdoor communities in south India. Started primarily as a trekking club, over the span of few years it has expanded itself to accommodate a wide range of activities. They range from mountain cycling to triathlons to exploratory missions in jungles to marathon to photography to biking to fitness. Not only the members enjoy the outdoors but also they have  developed a deep love and understanding for the nature and society in general. This has triggered a series of various social and environmental initiatives by CTC like Chennai Coastal Cleanup, lake cleanup activities,fort restoration, tree plantation, taking underprivileged children to trekking, celebrating festivals with them and others. It also organizes regular free workshops on running, cycling, photography, first aid, snakes etc.

 

Chennai Trail Marathon.

 

The Chennai Trail Marathon is another such initiative from CTC to give the running enthusiasts a chance to run on a trail amongst nature instead of tar roads. It's one of its kind in Chennai, all other marathons being run on hard tar roads. The first edition of trail marathon last year witnessed around 510 participants and it was a huge success. This year with early registration opening and proper planning, participation of around 1000-1500 participants is expected. The participants will include running enthusiasts from Chennai  and other cities, CTC members, kids, and a large variety of people. Around 2000-3000 members are expected to come there and support the runners. The event will be covered widely in media. Hence any organization associating with CTC will greatly benefit from it.

 

 

Frequently Asked Questions

 Before you shoot us e-mail or call us for clarifications. We would suggest you to read the FAQ’s. However, if you still have doubts we are ready and willing to help you.

What is Chennai Trail Marathon?

 Trail Marathon’s within city limits are unknown experience in our city’s history. Chennai Trekking Club (CTC) conducted the first trail marathon in October 2012, which was a history by itself since it was Chennai’s first ever ‘Trail Marathon’ unique from running on Tar roads in concrete jungle.

 

 We introduce to you trails along scenic routes in the outskirt of Chennai city to enjoy Running in natural atmosphere.

 

 Who is CTC? What is the level of Professionalimd you can expect in this Marathon?

  CTC is a volunteer driven largest outdoor club in South India that almost treks in every weekend of the year. Rain or Shine it easy to spot our members camping in some forest of South India. We believe ‘there is pleasure in the pathless woods’

 

 To know more about Chennai Trekking Club, please visitwww.chennaitrekkers.org

 

 This is the Second Edition of CTC’s trail marathon. We have learnt a lot of lessons from our previous editions and from our colleagues Chennai Runners and Dream Runners.

 

 The entire Marathon will be run by selfless volunteers of the Club right from pulling the mile markers till obtaining permission from police personnel this event is a complete volunteer driven event. Therefore, you can expect selfless service with unbound love and affection from this event.

 

 Last date for registrations:

 We intend to close the registrations on 8th October 2013.

 

 

Running Trail and Elevation Profile:

 The Running trail will be notified few weeks before the event along with the elevation profile.

 

 

BIB Collection:

 One day prior to the Race day you or your authorised representative can collect the BIB’s. The place of collection and other particulars will be intimated to the registered e-mail i.d.

 

 

Refund of Race Fee:

 The Race fee is non-refundable.

 

 

Transfer of Registration / BIB’s:

 You may transfer the Registration / BIB’s upon prior intimation to the Organizers. However, we will not guarantee correct completion certificate in the name of the transferee.

 

 Change of Category:

 Change of category from Full marathon to half marathon and Half marathon to full marathon is possible upon prior intimation to the Organizers.

 

 

Spot registration possibility:

 Will be happening on  Saturday, Oct 19th @ Hotel HABLIS, Guindy, Chennai.

Entry Fee Will be 600/- Per Person for Adults on All Category.

Entry For Kids 400/- Per Kid.

T-Shirts for Spot Registrations completely depends upon stock availability.

 

 

 

KIDS RUN:

 All runners less than 12 years of age shall be represented by their Parent or Guardian and only on obtaining written consent of their respective Parent or Guardian, before the Bib collection date, they shall be permitted to participate in the marathon.

 

 

Student RUN:

 College/School/Institution ID is must at the time of collecting your BIB Number and race kit.

 

 

Baggage Storage:

 A baggage storage counter will be operative during the official course of the event.

 

 

Medals / Finishers certificates

 Every finisher will get a finisher medal at the finish line and finisher certificate will be uploaded online. For Kids Run the certificates will be provided at the venue.

 

 We will NOT be providing any cash Prize for participants who finish in the top positions of their respective category.

 

 

 Breakfast:

 We will provide you with breakfast.

 

 

Car / Two Wheeler / Cycle Parking:

 Provision for Car, two-wheeler and cycle parking will be made available.

 

 

Car Pooling:

 We will make efforts to arrange for car-pooling from few common destinations (Koyambedu, Guindy, Tirunvanmyur, Adayar) only for participants coming from Chennai.
